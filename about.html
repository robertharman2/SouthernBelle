About Southern Belle Sweets

Southern Belle Sweets is a locally owned (Allendale, MI) gourmet sweets shop made famous for our Hot Chocolate Bombs.

The homemade, cupcake sized hot chocolate bomb is filled with hot chocolate mix and marshmallows. Simply drop in a mug of hot milk or water, stir, and watch the magic happen!
